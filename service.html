What we do

Sound Therapy for Body, Mind & Soul

Sonal's Practice

Sonal is a trained sound healing practitioner and Nada Yoga guide, offering sessions with compassion, clarity, and grounded presence. Each session is held in a safe, inclusive, and respectful environment.

Our Philosophy

Aumkara represents transformation through awareness.

At Aumkara, sound is used not to fix, but to reveal the natural intelligence of the body and mind.

We honor:

  • Sound as sacred
  • Silence as medicine
  • Presence as the true healer

Who Can Attend?

Sound healing is suitable for all - beginners and experienced practitioners alike. No prior experience is needed. Simply come as you are and receive.

Private Sound Healing Sessions

Personalized one-on-one sessions designed to support relaxation, emotional release, grounding, and energetic alignment.

Group Sound Baths

Studio or at-home group events invite participants into a peaceful collective journey of relaxation, balance, and inner quiet.

Corporate Sessions

Corporate sound bath sessions create a restorative pause that helps teams de-stress, reset, and return with renewed energy.
