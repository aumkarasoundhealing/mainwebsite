What Is Sound Healing?

Sound healing is a holistic practice that uses frequency and vibration to support physical, emotional, and energetic balance.

In the tradition of Nāda Yoga, sound is not merely heard — it is experienced as consciousness itself. Through instruments such as singing bowls, gongs, bells, drums, and the human voice, sound gently guides the mind into rest and the body into restoration.
